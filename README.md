**Kwame Thomas**

---

## 🧭 Project Overview

This project presents a **90-day implementation plan and governance framework** for responsible AI adoption across:

* North Carolina Department of Information Technology (NCDIT)
* North Carolina Department of Public Instruction (NC DPI)
* Public sector agencies and K–12 school systems

The goal is to ensure AI systems are:

* Ethical
* Secure
* Compliant
* Equitable
* Practical for real-world use

---

## 🎯 Problem Statement

Public sector organizations—especially education—are adopting AI tools without:

* Standard governance frameworks
* Risk assessment processes
* Training for safe use
* Transparency for stakeholders

This creates risks in:

* Student data privacy (FERPA)
* Algorithmic bias
* Vendor AI misuse
* Inconsistent adoption across districts

---

## 🧠 Solution

A **3-phase AI Governance Implementation Strategy (90 Days)**

---

## 📅 Phase 1: Discovery (Days 1–30)

### Deliverables:

* AI Stakeholder Map
* AI Use Case Register
* Top 10 AI Risk Report

### Key Risks:

* Bias in AI grading tools
* Student data exposure
* Lack of transparency
* Shadow AI usage

---

## 📅 Phase 2: Framework & Pilot (Days 31–60)

### Core Components:

#### AI Risk Classification

* Low Risk → Productivity tools
* Medium Risk → Student support tools
* High Risk → Decision-making systems

---

#### AI Risk Heatmap

| Risk | Data Sensitivity | Impact | Example          |
| ---- | ---------------- | ------ | ---------------- |
| Low  | Low              | Low    | Lesson generator |
| Med  | Moderate         | Medium | AI tutor         |
| High | High (PII)       | High   | Grading AI       |

---

#### Governance Controls

* Human-in-the-loop
* Vendor transparency
* Data protection
* Bias checks

---

### Pilot:

* 2–3 school districts
* 1 state agency

---

## 📅 Phase 3: Scale (Days 61–90)

### Deliverables:

* AI Policy & Playbook
* AI Literacy Training Program
* Governance Operating Model

---

## 📊 Expected Outcomes

* Full visibility into AI usage
* Reduced risk exposure
* FERPA compliance alignment
* Standardized AI adoption
* Increased educator confidence
